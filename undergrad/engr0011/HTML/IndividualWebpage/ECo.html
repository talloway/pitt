What types of companies hire computer engineers?

  1. Nvidia

    Nvidia is a technology company that designs graphics processing units (GPUs) for gaming and professional markets, as well as for cryptocurrency. The company is headquartered in Santa Clara, CA.

      Products offered:
    • Tegra, a mobile super chip that is rooted in visual and parallel computing. Used in phones, in-car safety devics, and infotainment systems.
    • GeForce, a GPU made specifically for PC gaming, a currently booming market.
    • Tesla, a programmable GPU that is used by scientists and researchers to model complex problems. Powers three of top five of world's supercomputers.

      Services offered:
    • Developer Zone, a collection of information useful for GPU programmers
    • CUDA support, an informational network for CUDA, a platform for computing on a GPU

    The work environment at Nvidia pays significant emphasis to the well-being of the company's employees. Nvidia offers a number of benefits to help handle stress, such as professional time off and on-site relaxation services. Employees make upwards of $110,000 per year at Nvidia, and the work hours are generally normal, with 40 hour work weeks being the standard.

  2. Airbnb

    Airbnb is an online hospitality service where people can lease or rent short-term housing. It is used for vacation rentals, apartment rentals, and as hotel rooms. It is headquartered in San Francisco, CA.

      Products offered:
    • Airbnb app: User-friendly and very interactive, allows people to conveniently book accomodations for themselves.

      Services offered:
    • Neighborhood matching system that will match a traveler to an area where they will feel most comfortable.
    • Guidebooks, a platform that gives travelers information about the area in which they will be staying, such as the best restaurants and attractions.

    Airbnb is a challenging place to work at, as it is not a fully established company at only nine years old. It provides a comfortable space for its employees, ensuring they feel welcomed in its culture. Salaries are generally upwards of $120,000 yearly, and the work hours are mostly as expected.
